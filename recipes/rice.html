Wali wa nazi

Description

Wali wa Nazi, also known as Coconut Rice, is a flavorful dish enjoyed in East Africa.

Ingridients

  • 2 cups of long-grain white rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 3-4 whole cloves (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse the Rice:
    Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch.


  2. Prepare the Coconut Mixture:
    In a mixing bowl, combine the coconut milk and water. Mix well until the coconut milk is evenly distributed.


  3. Sauté the Rice:
    In a large pot or saucepan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is lightly toasted.


  4. Add Coconut Mixture:
    Pour the coconut milk and water mixture over the toasted rice. Stir in salt and add the cinnamon stick and whole cloves if using.


  5. Bring to a Boil:
    Bring the mixture to a gentle boil


  6. Reduce Heat and Simmer:
    Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.


  7. Check for Doneness:
    Check the rice for doneness. If it's still a bit firm, you can add a little more water and continue to simmer until fully cooked.


  8. Fluff and Serve:
    Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork to separate the grains. Remove the cinnamon stick and whole cloves if used.


  9. Serve Warm:
    Serve the Wali wa Nazi warm as a side dish, pairing it with your favorite stews, grilled meats, or vegetables.


For extra richness, you can add a few tablespoons of coconut cream to the cooking liquid. You can also add a bit of sugar if you prefer a slightly sweet flavor. Wali wa Nazi is a delicious accompaniment to various dishes and is often enjoyed as part of traditional East African meals. Adjust the seasoning and ingredients to suit your taste preferences.
